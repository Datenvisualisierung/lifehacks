Bayer MaterialScience – a leader in Germany and worldwide

We are one of the world’s leading suppliers of materials: innovative, sustainable and versatile. In keeping with the Group mission statement “Bayer – Science For A Better Life,” we are working on solutions to the challenges of our time – fuel-efficient cars, energy-efficient buildings, clean technologies. Germany is the company’s home country. 5,200 people work here – more than one-third of the global workforce of 14,500.

State-of-the-art technology is used to make products of the highest safety standards at the four major sites: Brunsbüttel, Dormagen, Krefeld-Uerdingen and Leverkusen.
